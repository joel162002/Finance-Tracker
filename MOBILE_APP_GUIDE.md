## 📲 How to Install on Your Phone

### **For Android (Chrome/Samsung Internet)**

1. Open your browser and go to your app URL:  
   `https://kita-currency-fix.preview.emergentagent.com`

2. Log in with your credentials:
   - Email: `demo@finance.com`
   - Password: `demo123`

3. You'll see an "Install App" popup at the bottom - tap **Install**

   **OR**

4. Tap the **3-dot menu** (⋮) in the browser
5. Select **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**
6. Tap **"Add"** or **"Install"**
7. The app icon will appear on your home screen!

### **For iPhone/iPad (Safari)**

1. Open **Safari** and go to your app URL:  
   `https://kita-currency-fix.preview.emergentagent.com`

2. Log in with your credentials

3. Tap the **Share button** (□↑) at the bottom
4. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Edit the name if you want (e.g., "Finance Tracker")
6. Tap **"Add"** in the top right
7. The app icon will appear on your home screen!

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**App not installing?**
- Make sure you're using Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS)
- Check that you have enough storage space
- Try refreshing the page and trying again

**App not working offline?**
- Make sure you've opened the app at least once with internet
- The app needs to cache data on first use

**Not seeing the install prompt?**
- You may have dismissed it earlier
- Clear your browser data and try again
- Or use the "Add to Home Screen" method manually
